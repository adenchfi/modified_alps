Mailing List

We offer the following mailing lists related to the libraries in the ALPS project:

  • Announcements of new releases: comp-phys-alps-announce@lists.comp-phys.org
  • Discussion and questions regarding the use of the ALPS libraries: comp-phys-alps-users@lists.comp-phys.org
  • Discussion of the ALPS libraries and their development: [https://webmail.phys.ethz.ch/mailman/listinfo/comp-phys-alps-devel comp-phys-alps-devel@lists.comp-phys.org]
